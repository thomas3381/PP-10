How to Cancel a PayPal Payment

If you’ve made a PayPal payment by mistake, here’s how to cancel it and get your refund.

1. Can I Cancel a PayPal Payment?

If your payment is still pending (i.e., it hasn't been completed yet), you can cancel it directly from your PayPal account:

  • Log into your PayPal account and go to the Activity section.
  • Find the pending payment and click on it.
  • If the payment is still pending, you'll have the option to cancel it.

2. Requesting a Refund

If the payment has been completed and the transaction was made to a merchant, you'll need to request a refund:

  • Go to the Resolution Center on PayPal.
  • Click on Report a Problem and select the transaction you want refunded.
  • Follow the steps to submit your dispute or request a refund.

3. Get Help from PayPal Customer Support

If you encounter any problems with canceling a payment or requesting a refund, contact PayPal customer service:

  • Call PayPal at +1 800-891-9140 for help.
  • Use live chat for immediate assistance.
  • Visit the PayPal Help Center for more information on canceling payments.
